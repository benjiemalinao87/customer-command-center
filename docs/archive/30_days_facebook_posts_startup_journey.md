## Tips for Posting:
1. Post at consistent times (e.g., 9 AM or 6 PM)
2. Engage with comments - respond authentically
3. Use relevant hashtags (3-5 per post)
4. Include visuals when possible (screenshots, code snippets, diagrams)
5. Be authentic - share real struggles and wins
6. Tag relevant people/companies when appropriate
7. Cross-post to LinkedIn if relevant
8. Track engagement to see what resonates

## Content Variations:
- Mix personal stories with Naval's concepts
- Alternate between technical and business insights
- Include questions to drive engagement
- Share specific numbers and metrics
- Use quotes from Naval Ravikant when relevant
- Share behind-the-scenes moments
- Celebrate small wins
- Be vulnerable about challenges
